SDG GOAL 4: QUALITY EDUCATION

SDG 4 focuses on providing quality education for all. It promotes equal learning opportunities regardless of gender, location, or background. Quality education helps individuals gain knowledge, skills, and values needed to improve their lives and communities.

• Ensure free and quality primary and secondary education for all.
• Equal access to affordable higher education and technical training.
• Eliminate gender disparities in education.
• Increase literacy and numeracy skills among youth and adults.
• Build safe, inclusive, and effective learning environments.

Around 260 million children worldwide were out of school in 2018. In the Philippines, while many students attend school, challenges remain in ensuring that all learners receive quality education, especially in rural areas.

• Lack of learning materials and school facilities
• Poverty and child labor
• Gender inequality
• Lack of trained teachers
• Impact of the COVID-19 pandemic

Governments, organizations, and communities can work together by:

• Providing scholarships and free education programs
• Supporting teacher training and better facilities
• Promoting digital and inclusive learning
• Encouraging community participation

• Support local schools
• Donate books or materials
• Volunteer to teach or tutor
• Spread awareness about SDG 4
